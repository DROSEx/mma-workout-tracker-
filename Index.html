MMA Athlete Workout Plan (Age 33)

6 days training • 1 rest day • 60-90 min sessions • Warm-up 5-10 min light cardio + dynamic stretches • Cool-down stretch/foam roll

Day 1 & 4: Lower Body Strength + Core

1. Squats (barbell or goblet) – Target: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
2. Romanian Deadlifts – Target: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
3. Lunges (walking or reverse, dumbbell) – Target: 3 sets of 10 per leg
4. Calf Raises – Target: 3 sets of 12–15 reps
5. Core circuit (3 rounds): Planks (60 sec), Russian Twists (20 per side), Hanging Leg Raises (10–12)

Day 2 & 5: Upper Body Strength + Striking Conditioning

1. Bench Press (barbell or dumbbell) – Target: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
2. Bent-Over Rows – Target: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
3. Overhead Press (dumbbell) – Target: 3 sets of 8–10 reps
4. Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldowns – Target: 3 sets of 8–12 reps
5. 20–30 min Boxing Circuit or BODYCOMBAT class

Day 3 & 6: Full-Body HIIT / Conditioning or Mobility

Circuit (4–5 rounds, 45s work / 15s rest): Burpees, Kettlebell Swings, Shadow Boxing (3-min rounds), Battle Ropes or Med Ball Slams, Mountain Climbers
+ 15–20 min cycle/rower + Reform Pilates or Hot Yoga (Day 6 focus)

Day 7: Rest or light active recovery

Use Richmond Platinum for energy/recovery, QV or Flinders for 24/7 or boxing focus. Track progressive overload every 2–3 weeks.
